Alpaca Wellness from Exmoor’s Natural Pastures
British Alpaca Fashion’s journey begins on Weekfield Farm in Exmoor National Park where we believe in keeping things as close to nature as possible. Our alpacas roam freely in pastures untouched by chemicals, enjoying lush grass and hay we grow ourselves. Each one of our alpacas is special to us; they all have names and a place in our hearts, reminding us that quality begins with care. When we gather their fleece each year, we do it with respect, ensuring each animal’s wellness is central to our process.
British Alpaca wellness and premium fibre development
For us, wellness isn’t just for our alpacas—it is woven into every fibre of our garments. We see the results of our approach in the fibre’s strength, softness, and warmth. This natural, hypoallergenic fleece is as gentle on the skin as it is versatile. Available in a range of natural colours, it reflects our commitment to creating clothing that’s as kind to the planet as it is to the people who wear it.
Advanced knitting technology at Exmoor Micro factory
In our Exmoor Micro factory, tradition meets modern innovation. We use advanced knitting technology powered largely by solar energy, allowing us to create garments that last with minimal impact on the environment. For us, each stitch matters, bringing to life our vision of responsible, slow fashion—pieces that will stand the test of time and trend.
Artisanal Finishing with Pure Spring Water
Each garment receives careful hand-finished touches, enhanced by using pure spring water from our own source on the farm. This gentle process adds exceptional quality to each piece, preserving the fibre’s natural feel and reducing environmental impact. To us, this step is more than just finishing a garment—it’s a way to honor the integrity of each piece and the land that sustains us.
Natural Wellness in every wear for the consumer
Alpaca fibre offers warmth, breathability, and hypoallergenic properties. When you wear one of our pieces, you experience the warmth and comfort of alpaca fibre, and you are wearing something made with intention. This natural fibre is lightweight, hypoallergenic, breathable, and temperature-regulating, bringing comfort and ease to everyday wear. Our hope is that each piece brings wellness into your life, wrapping you in softness and a bit of Exmoor’s natural beauty. The apparel reflects British Alpaca Fashion’s goal of making clothes that are naturally suited to both skin and lifestyle.
Repurpose, Redesign and Sustain
We design our garments to be cherished for a lifetime and beyond. From the way they are crafted to the thoughtful details, each piece is made with the idea that it could be repurposed or redesigned over time. This approach to circular fashion is part of our commitment to sustainability, encouraging us—and you—to value what we wear and appreciate its journey.
